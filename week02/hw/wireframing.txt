The purpose of wireframing is to help stakeholders get an idea of how a website 
is going to function. It's good practice to communicate with stakeholders and
customers frequently is possible and to ask questions. When designing wireframes, 
we should keep in mind who our intended audience is and where we want things to go 
and how they should flow. Sketches should be simple, and we can use tools to create
wireframes of varying complexity based on our audience.
